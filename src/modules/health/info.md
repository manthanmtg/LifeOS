# Health

A centralized vault for medical history, prescriptions, vaccinations, and health tracking for family members and pets.

## Features

- Per-member health profiles (self, family, pets)
- Track conditions, allergies, and chronic issues
- Medication management with refill alerts
- Vaccination timeline with overdue alerts
- Doctor visit / appointment log with cost tracking
- Lab results with trend indicators and status color-coding
- Height/weight/BMI tracking over time
- Health document organization (prescriptions, bills, lab reports, etc.)

## How to Use

Create a profile for each family member or pet. Add their medical details — allergies, conditions, medications, and vaccination history. Log every doctor visit and lab result to build a complete health record. Track height and weight over time to monitor BMI trends. Store documents like prescriptions and lab reports for easy reference.

## Tips

- Keep medication refill dates updated so you get timely alerts
- Log vaccination next-due dates to never miss a booster
- Record lab results consistently to spot trends over time
- Use tags to group profiles (e.g., "household", "parents")
