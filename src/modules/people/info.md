# People

A personal CRM to manage your contacts, remember birthdays, and track interactions.

## Features

- Store contacts with name, phone, email, and relationship type
- Track birthdays and important dates
- Log interactions and conversations (met, called, messaged, etc.)
- Add personal notes about people (interests, family details, context)
- Search and filter contacts by relationship type or recency of interaction

## How to Use

Add people you want to stay in touch with. Fill in their details, birthday, and how you know them. After meaningful interactions, log a quick note about what you discussed. Use the module to review who you haven't reached out to in a while and plan catch-ups.

## Tips

- Log interactions right after they happen while details are fresh
- Add context notes (e.g., "loves hiking, has two kids") to personalize future conversations
- Review the list monthly to spot relationships that need nurturing
