# Life OS — Module Ideas

This document maintains an exhaustive list of potential future modules for the **Life OS** ecosystem. Each module follows the standard `AdminView` + `Widget` + `Schema` contract.

---

## 🧘 Health & Wellness

### 1. Daily Journal / Reflection
*   **What it is:** A per-day markdown journal for long-form thoughts and mood tracking.
*   **Features:** Calendar navigation, mood selector (1-10 or emoji), "On this day" flashback.
*   **Example:** "Today was a P1 day. I finally finished the EMI Tracker logic and felt great about the progress."

### 2. Fitness & Workout Logger
*   **What it is:** Track gym sessions, sports, or home workouts.
*   **Features:** Exercise library, set/rep/weight logging, volume charts.
*   **Example:** "Deadlift: 3 sets x 5 reps @ 140kg."

### 3. Sleep & Recovery Tracker
*   **What it is:** Monitor sleep quality, duration, and energy levels.
*   **Features:** Sleep score charts, caffeine intake vs sleep quality correlation.
*   **Example:** "Sleep: 7h 20m, Quality: 85%. Felt groggy after 3 coffess post 4 PM."

### 4. Meditation & Mindfulness
*   **What it is:** Log meditation sessions and mindfulness practices.
*   **Features:** Duration timer, focus area (breathing, body scan), streak tracking.
*   **Example:** "10m Mindful Breathing. Focus was a bit scattered today."

## 💰 Finance & Wealth

### 5. Net Worth Tracker
*   **What it is:** A high-level view of assets and liabilities over time.
*   **Features:** Account categories (Cash, Stocks, Crypto, Real Estate), monthly snapshots.
*   **Example:** "Total Assets: $250k. Liabilities: $80k (Home Loan). Net Worth: $170k (+5% this month)."

### 6. Investment Ledger
*   **What it is:** Detailed tracking of stock/crypto purchases and performance.
*   **Features:** Buy/Sell logs, P&L calculations, portfolio diversification donut chart.
*   **Example:** "Buy 10 shares of AAPL @ $185. Total position 50 shares."

### 7. Tax Assistant
*   **What it is:** Organize tax-deductible expenses and income documents.
*   **Features:** Document upload (receipts/forms), category tagging for tax season.
*   **Example:** "Tag June 15th subscription as 'Business Expense (Software)'."

### 8. Wishlist & Priority Buying
*   **What it is:** A more intentional shopping list for high-value items.
*   **Features:** Cost, urgency (P1-P5), link, reasoning ("Why do I need this?").
*   **Example:** "New Ergonomic Chair - $600 - P2. Current chair causes back pain."

## ⚡ Productivity & Knowledge

### 9. Goals & OKR Tracker
*   **What it is:** Hierarchical goal setting with measurable key results.
*   **Features:** Progress bars, parent/child goals, deadlines.
*   **Example:** "Objective: Run a Marathon. KR: Complete a 30km run by August 1st (70% complete)."

### 10. Personal Wiki / Zettelkasten
*   **What it is:** A structured knowledge base for notes, research, and permanent ideas.
*   **Features:** Bidirectional linking, tags, folder-less structure.
*   **Example:** "Note on 'Atomic Habits' linked to 'Habit Tracker Module Architecture'."

### 11. Flashcards / Spaced Repetition
*   **What it is:** Learn new topics using Anki-style repetition.
*   **Features:** Question/Answer cards, difficulty rating, daily review queue.
*   **Example:** "Card: How does JWT work? Review in 4 days."

### 12. Pomodoro & Focus Timer
*   **What it is:** Integrated timer for deep work sessions.
*   **Features:** Session logging, module linking ("I spent 4 slots on Blog").
*   **Example:** "Today: 8 Pomodoros completed (4h Deep Work)."

## 🌍 Lifestyle & Hobbies

### 13. Travel Planner
*   **What it is:** Manage itinerarties, bookings, and packing lists.
*   **Features:** Day-by-day schedule, PDF attachment for tickets, currency converter.
*   **Example:** "Trip to Japan: Sept 10-25. Flight QR123. Stay: Kyoto Ryokan."

### 14. Recipe Book & Meal Planner
*   **What it is:** Store personal recipes and schedule weekly meals.
*   **Features:** Ingredient list, instructions, link to Shopping List module.
*   **Example:** "Monday Dinner: Grilled Salmon (ingredients added to shopping list)."

### 15. Gaming Library
*   **What it is:** Track games played, completion status, and personal ratings.
*   **Features:** Platform (Steam, PS5), hours played, "Currently Playing" state.
*   **Example:** "Elden Ring: Complete (10/10). 85 hours played."

### 16. Wine / Coffee / Tasting Log
*   **What it is:** Detailed sensory notes for specific interests.
*   **Features:** Origin, roast/vintage, notes, rating.
*   **Example:** "Ethiopia Yirgacheffe: Light roast, floral notes, high acidity."

## 🤝 Relationships & Networking

### 17. Personal CRM (People)
*   **What it is:** Keep track of important life contacts and interactions.
*   **Features:** Birthday reminders, "Last Contacted" date, shared interests/notes.
*   **Example:** "Friend: Rahul. Not contacted in 3 months. Reminder: He's moving to Austin soon."

### 18. Gift Idea Tracker
*   **What it is:** Store gift ideas for people year-round.
*   **Features:** Recipient, occasion, price range, link.
*   **Example:** "Anniversary gift: Hand-thrown ceramic set. Seen at local market."

## 🏠 Household & Logistics

### 19. Inventory & Asset Manager
*   **What it is:** Track physical belongings, serial numbers, and warranties.
*   **Features:** Purchase date, price, photo/document upload, expiry alerts.
*   **Example:** "MacBook Pro M3. Serial: XXXXX. Warranty expires Dec 2026."

### 20. Home Maintenance Log
*   **What it is:** Recurring tasks for keeping your space in order.
*   **Features:** AC service, water filter changes, insurance renewals.
*   **Example:** "Replace AC filter every 6 months. Last replaced: Jan 2026."

### 21. Car / Vehicle Manager
*   **What it is:** Log mileage, fuel, and service history.
*   **Features:** Service records, insurance/pollution certificate expiry alerts.
*   **Example:** "Oil change @ 45,000km. Cost: $120. Next service: 55,000km."

### 22. Health
*   **What it is:** A centralized vault for medical history, prescriptions, and health tracking for the family and pets.
*   **Features:** Document storage (bills/prescriptions), vaccination schedules, recurring checkup reminders, doctor notes.

---

## 🤖 Advanced AI & Data

### 23. Voice-to-Action
*   **What it is:** Record audio notes that AI automatically parses into other modules.
*   **Features:** "Log expense 50 dollars for lunch" → automatically creates record.

### 23. Life Insights (Predictive)
*   **What it is:** AI-driven analysis across all modules.
*   **Features:** "You tend to skip Meditating on days when your Expense count is high." (Correlation between stress and finance).
