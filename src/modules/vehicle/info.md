# Vehicle

Track vehicle service history, fuel logs, and document expiry dates for all your vehicles.

## Features

- Log service records with date, type, cost, and odometer reading
- Track fuel fills with quantity, cost, and mileage calculation
- Document expiry reminders (insurance, registration, PUC, etc.)
- Support multiple vehicles
- Service cost history and fuel efficiency trends over time

## How to Use

Add your vehicle(s) with basic details. Log every service visit and fuel fill to build a complete maintenance history. Add document expiry dates so you get reminded before renewals are due. Over time, the fuel log helps you track mileage trends and spot efficiency drops that might indicate maintenance needs.

## Tips

- Log fuel fills every time to get accurate mileage tracking
- Set document expiry dates immediately after renewal so you never miss one
- Record even minor services (tire pressure, top-ups) for a complete vehicle history
