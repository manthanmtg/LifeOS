# Compass

A Kanban-style project and task board for managing larger goals and multi-step work.

## Features

- Kanban columns (Backlog, In Progress, Done, etc.) with drag-and-drop
- Create tasks with title, description, priority, and due dates
- Organize tasks into projects or areas
- Filter by priority, status, or project
- Track progress across multiple ongoing initiatives

## How to Use

Create columns that match your workflow (e.g., Backlog, Up Next, In Progress, Done). Add tasks with enough detail to act on them. Drag tasks between columns as you make progress. Use priority levels to focus on what matters most. For bigger projects, break work into multiple smaller tasks.

## Tips

- Keep "In Progress" limited to 2-3 items to avoid context-switching
- Review and groom the backlog weekly to keep it relevant
- Move completed items to Done rather than deleting — it's motivating to see progress
