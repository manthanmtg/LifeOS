# Reading

A reading queue for articles, papers, videos, and other online content you want to consume later.

## Features

- Save links with title, URL, and content type (article, paper, video, podcast, etc.)
- Mark items as unread, in-progress, or completed
- Tag and categorize items for easy retrieval
- Filter by status to see your backlog or review what you've read
- Add personal notes after consuming content

## How to Use

When you come across something worth reading or watching later, add it here with the URL and a brief description. Work through your queue by filtering for unread items. After finishing, mark it complete and optionally add notes capturing key takeaways.

## Tips

- Keep the queue manageable — if something has been unread for months, consider removing it
- Add notes immediately after reading while insights are fresh
- Use tags to group related content for deep-dive sessions on a topic
