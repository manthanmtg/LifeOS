# Ideas

A quick-capture brainstorming board for ideas, shower thoughts, and inspiration.

## Features

- Quickly jot down ideas with a title and description
- Tag ideas by category (project, business, creative, etc.)
- Mark ideas with priority or status (raw, exploring, in-progress, parked)
- Search and filter through your idea archive
- No structure required — just dump and go

## How to Use

Whenever an idea strikes, open this module and capture it before it fades. A short title and a few sentences is enough. Come back later to flesh out promising ones, tag them, or link them to projects in the Compass module. The goal is zero friction for capture.

## Tips

- Don't self-censor when capturing — write everything down, filter later
- Review your ideas list monthly to spot patterns or revisit forgotten gems
- Move actionable ideas to Compass or Todo when you're ready to execute
