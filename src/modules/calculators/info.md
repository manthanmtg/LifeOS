# Calculators

A collection of financial calculators to help you plan investments and compare returns.

## Features

- SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) calculator with projected growth
- Fixed Deposit calculator with maturity amount
- Lumpsum investment calculator
- EMI calculator for quick loan estimates
- Compare different scenarios side by side

## How to Use

Select the calculator type you need, enter the required values (amount, rate, tenure), and view the results instantly. Adjust the inputs to compare different scenarios — for example, see how increasing your monthly SIP by a small amount affects the final corpus over 10 years.

## Tips

- Use the SIP calculator to set realistic long-term savings goals
- Compare FD returns against SIP to understand the power of compounding in equity
- Try different tenures to find the sweet spot between EMI affordability and total interest paid
