# Expenses

Track your daily spending across categories to understand where your money goes.

## Features

- Log expenses with amount, category, date, and optional notes
- Categorize spending (food, transport, utilities, entertainment, etc.)
- View monthly and category-wise breakdowns
- Visual charts showing spending patterns over time
- Filter and search through past expenses

## How to Use

Add a new expense by clicking the add button. Enter the amount, pick a category, and optionally add a note describing the purchase. Expenses default to today's date but you can backdate them. Use the filters to view expenses by month or category and spot trends in your spending.

## Tips

- Be consistent with categories to get meaningful breakdowns
- Add expenses as they happen so you don't forget smaller purchases
- Review your monthly summary at month-end to identify areas to cut back
