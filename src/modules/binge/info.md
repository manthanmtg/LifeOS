# Binge

Track movies, series, documentaries, and anime in one place.

Log what you're watching, what's queued up, and what you've completed. Rate titles, track series progress by season and episode, and note who recommended what.

**Features:**
- Track movies, series, documentaries, and anime
- Statuses: To Watch / Watching / Completed / Dropped
- Rating (1–10), notes, genre, platform
- Series progress: current season & episode
- Rewatch tracking
- Filter by status and type, search by title
