# Portfolio

Your public-facing profile page showcasing who you are, your work, and your skills.

## Features

- Customizable personal bio and introduction
- Showcase projects, skills, and experience
- Social media and contact links
- Public by default — serves as your personal landing page
- Clean, responsive design for sharing with others

## How to Use

Edit your portfolio content from the admin view. Add your bio, list your key projects or work, and include relevant links. The portfolio is rendered as a public page that you can share as your personal website. Update it whenever you have new projects or accomplishments to highlight.

## Tips

- Keep the bio concise and focused — 2-3 sentences that capture who you are and what you do
- Highlight your best 3-5 projects rather than listing everything
- Include links to live projects, GitHub repos, or relevant profiles
