# Bookshelf

Track your book collection with reading progress, ratings, and personal notes.

## Features

- Add books with title, author, genre, and cover image
- Track reading status: to-read, currently reading, finished, abandoned
- Log reading progress (pages or percentage)
- Rate and review books after finishing
- Filter by status, genre, or author

## How to Use

Add books as you discover them, setting their status to "to-read." When you start a book, update its status and log your progress as you go. Once finished, rate it and jot down your thoughts. Use the shelves to browse your collection and decide what to read next.

## Tips

- Update progress regularly to build a satisfying reading history
- Don't hesitate to mark a book as abandoned — life's too short for bad books
- Use the "to-read" list as your personal recommendation queue
