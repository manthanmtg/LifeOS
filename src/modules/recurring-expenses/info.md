# Recurring Expenses

Track subscriptions, memberships, and recurring bills in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

## Features

- Log recurring expenses with amount, frequency (monthly, yearly, etc.), and category
- Track active vs. paused vs. cancelled subscriptions
- See total monthly and yearly recurring cost at a glance
- Track renewal dates and billing cycles
- Categorize by type (streaming, SaaS, insurance, utilities, etc.)

## How to Use

Add each subscription or recurring bill with its amount and billing frequency. Mark subscriptions as active or cancelled as your usage changes. The dashboard shows your total recurring burn rate so you can see the real cost of all those "just $5/month" services.

## Tips

- Audit this list quarterly — cancel anything you haven't used recently
- Use the yearly view to see the true annual cost of all subscriptions combined
- Log even small recurring charges; they add up fast
